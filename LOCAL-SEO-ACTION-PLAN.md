## The ranking model you're optimizing for

Google's local pack ranks on three things: **Relevance** (does your GBP + site match the query), **Distance** (searcher → your service area), and **Prominence** (reviews, citations, links, web authority). GBP drives the pack; your website drives localized-organic *and* feeds the GBP's relevance/prominence. So we work both together.

10. **Reviews are the #1 lever after proximity.** Ask every past/current client (PackShip, NYMM, NYFF, Vintus, Landscape Drainage Proz, etc.) for a Google review with a direct review link. Target 5–10 in the first month, then a steady trickle. Respond to each one.
