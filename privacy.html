Privacy.

NeuraGul is a software development agency. This site exists so you can see our work and get in touch. We keep data collection to the minimum that makes that possible.

What we collect

The only information we collect is what you choose to send through the contact form: your name, email address, project type, and message. We don't run analytics, advertising trackers, or cookies on this site.

How it's used

Contact form submissions are delivered to us by Formspree, which processes the submission on our behalf and is subject to its own privacy policy. We use what you send us for one thing: replying to you about your inquiry. We don't sell, rent, or share your information with anyone else, and we don't add you to mailing lists.

Retention

We keep correspondence for as long as it's relevant to an active or potential engagement. If you'd like your information deleted, email us and we'll remove it.

Third-party links

Our portfolio links to client sites and app stores. Once you leave neuragul.com, those sites' own privacy policies apply. Privacy policies for software products we build (such as PackShip) are published separately with those products.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data: support@neuragul.com.
